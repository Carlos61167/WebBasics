Environmental waste

Environmental waste has become one of the most pressing issues facing our planet today.

From plastic pollution in oceans to chemical runoff in rivers, waste generated by human activity is negatively impacting ecosystems, human health, and the global climate.
The effects are widespread and complex, touching nearly every aspect of life on Earth.

Here are a few major issues that are present in environmental waste:

  1. Environmental waste is ubiquitous,and is growing at an existential rate

  2. Environmental waste can also cause climate change.
